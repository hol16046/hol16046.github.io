Target Audience

The target audience will subsist of men and women most likely older than 18 who live in or are visiting the regions of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven and wish to know the weather conditions or local weather stories. They will most likely access the website from mobile devices, such as smartphones.

Personas

Ned West

  • Office Worker
  • Mixed Race, Age 52, Father of 4
  • Checks the weather to prepare for his communte to/from work.
  • Also gets weather information from the local news.
  • Frequently uses a smartphone, but also uses a tablet and computer.

Alison Smith

  • Life Coach
  • Caucasian, Age 28, Wife
  • Checks the weather to prepare for her commute and upcoming events.
  • Also gets weather information from her phone's preinstalled weather app.
  • Primarily uses a smartphone, uses a tablet occasionally.

Scenarios

  • Will I need to scrape my car windows this morning?
  • Is it going to freeze tonight?
  • Will it rain this weekend?
  • Should I expect snow on my commute?
  • What kind of weather should I expect on this weekend's camping trip?
