Holiday Weather Warning

By Staff

A winter storm warning is in effect for Southeastern Idaho until 5 AM MST on Thursday. Snow accumulations of 3 to 6 inches are expected in towns and on roads throughtout this region. Higher elevations are expected to receive 8 to 12 inches of snow. Wind gusts of 35+ mph will reduce driving visibility due to blowing and drifting snow. Holiday travel will be impacted, please exercise caution. If you are unaccustomed to driving in snow, we recommend this advice from @moorememesandjokes on Twitter. "Just pretend you're taking your grandma to church. There's a platter of biscuits and 2 gallons of sweet tea in glass jars in the back seat. She's wearing a new dress and holding a crock pot full of gravy." Have a wonderful and safe holiday!
